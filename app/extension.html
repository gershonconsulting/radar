Thanks for helping test Radar. This page walks you through installing the Radar Chrome extension, which quietly collects your Sales Navigator connections into your Radar dashboard.

⬇ Download the Radar extension
radar-extension-v1.5.3.zip · ~32 KB · Chrome / Edge (Chromium)

Before you start

You'll need Google Chrome (or Edge), a LinkedIn Sales Navigator account, and to have signed in once at radar.gershoncrm.com with LinkedIn.

Install (2 minutes)

  1. Download the zip above and unzip it. You'll get a folder named radar-extension.
  2. Open chrome://extensions in your browser.
  3. Turn on Developer mode (toggle, top-right).
  4. Click Load unpacked and select the unzipped radar-extension folder.
  5. Radar appears in your extensions. Click the puzzle-piece icon and pin it for easy access.
  6. Make sure you're logged into Sales Navigator in the same browser.
  7. Click the Radar icon → Collect Prospects Now (or Find New Bridges). It runs slowly in the background; results show up in your dashboard.
Please note: this is an early alpha. Run one collection per day and keep it in your own logged-in browser — the tool paces itself to stay gentle with LinkedIn. Your LinkedIn account use is your responsibility.

Updating

When a new version ships, download the new zip, replace your radar-extension folder, and click the ↺ reload icon on the Radar card in chrome://extensions.

Trouble?

If the icon does nothing, confirm you're signed into Sales Navigator and that Developer mode is on. Then reload the extension. Still stuck — email the Radar team.
