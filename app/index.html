How it works

Three stages. One warm path.

Cold outreach is a numbers game you lose. Radar plays a different one — it maps who already trusts you, and who trusts them.

1

SOURCEan organization

A place where your future clients already gather. A chamber, a trade body, a partner firm, even a competitor. You don't sell to a Source — you watch it, because the people you want are already in its orbit.

e.g. a chamber of commerce, an industry association, a partner firm
2

BRIDGEa person inside it

Someone inside it whose network overlaps your market. A partner, a board member, a director. Not your prospect either — they're the door. Radar sees who you already know, and invites the ones you don't.

e.g. a Partner there, connected to hundreds of founders in your market
3

TARGETa prospect

A real buyer inside a Bridge's network. Who you actually want to meet — reached via someone you both know, not a cold list. Graded A+ to D on real company data, then pushed to your outreach.

e.g. the CEO of a mid-sized company expanding into your market
